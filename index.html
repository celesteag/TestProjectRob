Who Are We?

RobEurope is a sustainable international robotic competition. The participating countries run their own competition with up to 6 teams, each team consisting of two or three students.

Competitions

The competions of all participating countries takes place at the same time with the same task. The competition tables of all partner countries are displayed at the walls arround the local table.
